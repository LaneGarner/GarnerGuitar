Easy songs

Songs are such a great way to learn new material. Not only will you grasp the information more quickly, but it will be fun at the same time! The repertoire list on pages 19-21 is a comprehensive resource to inspire you throughout your growth as a guitarist. Use the following song ideas to help you work on specific aspects of your playing as you are learning. Remember, these lists are just a starting point.
