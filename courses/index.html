Free Guitar Basics Course
Beginning to Intermediate Guitar Course 1

This intro course is designed to teach you the basics of guitar if you've never taken a lesson or even played before. You will learn foundational concepts with enough content to sustain long term growth as a player.

You will learn:

  • Guitar anatomy
  • Guitar accessories
  • Practice tips
  • Beginning music theory
  • Tuning
  • Finger names
  • Hand position
  • Starting chords
  • Tablature
  • Power chords
  • Chord transitions
  • Easy songs to learn
  • Rhythmic notation
  • The blues shuffle
  • Pentatonics
  • Open chords
  • Intro to barre chords
